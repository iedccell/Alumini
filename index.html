News and Updates

NSS Day Celebrated with 'Swatch e Seva' Initiative

Promoting Volunteerism at CEMP

On September 24, 2024, the College of Engineering and Management Punnapra (CEMP) observed NSS Day, emphasizing volunteerism and community service. The theme “Swatch e Seva” focused on cleanliness, with NSS volunteers assisting local sites.

Atharva Tech Fest 2024 Concludes at CEMP

A Unique Blend of Skills and Entertainment

The College of Engineering and Management, Punnapra, hosted the two-day Atharva Tech Fest 2024, featuring workshops on AI, robotics, and embedded systems. Attendees also enjoyed a gaming zone with tournaments and fun activities, fostering networking with professionals and alumni. The fest highlighted innovation and community engagement.

Pitch to Funding: Empowering Future Entrepreneurs at CEMP

Sparks Space Hosts Entrepreneurial Event

Sparks Space (IEDC) at CEMP held "Pitch to Funding" to boost students' entrepreneurial skills. Speakers Abidh Habeeb and Alen Anil shared insights on pitching and startup strategies. Students participated in hands-on exercises, receiving expert feedback to help transform their innovative ideas into successful startups
